About Me

I am driven by the questions medicine often avoids: why certain conditions remain understudied, why discomfort becomes stigma, and how research can be reshaped to center patients whose experiences fall outside traditional models. My work spans translational research, clinical collaboration, and mentorship, with the long-term goal of combining rigorous science with compassionate care.
