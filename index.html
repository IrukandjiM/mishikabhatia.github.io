About Me

I am a student researcher working at the intersection of biomedical research, clinical translation, and STEM equity. My work centers on diseases and questions that are common yet understudied, and conditions that are often dismissed due to stigma, complexity, or lack of funding.

Through independent projects, biomedical research, and hospital collaborations, I focus on asking questions that are overlooked and turning them into rigorous, patient-centered inquiry and accessible scientific research.
