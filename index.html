About Me

I am a student researcher working at the intersection of biomedical research, clinical translation, and health equity. My work centers on diseases that are common yet understudied—conditions often dismissed due to stigma, complexity, or lack of funding.

Through independent research, hospital collaborations, and nonprofit leadership, I focus on asking questions that sit at the margins of medicine and turning them into rigorous, patient-centered inquiry.
